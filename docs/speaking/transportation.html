Transportation
What&#39;s the most popular means of transportation in your hometown?

# Questions

  • What's the most popular means of transportation in your hometown?
  • How often do you take buses?
  • Can you compare the advantages of planes and trains?
  • Is driving to work popular in your country?
  • Do you think people will drive more in the future?

# Vocabulary

Transportation
  • somewhere
    • in or at or to some place
  • expensive
    • high in price or charging high prices
  • travelling
    • the act of going from one place to another
  • transportation
    • a facility consisting of the means and equipment necessary for the movement of passengers or goods
  • convenient
    • suited to your comfort or purpose or needs

# Answers

Here are some model answers!

TIP

Try to make up your own answers first!

  • What's the most popular means of transportation in your hometown?
    • Well, people in my hometown always seem to ride ebikes to go work or somewhere else because my hometown is a small town and it's very convenient to go out by ebike.
  • How often do you take buses?
    • I rarely go by bus because I have my own car now, so I always go somewhere by car.
  • Can you compare the advantages of planes and trains?
    • Well, between the two, travelling by plane is relatively very fast, for example, it just takes three hours for me to go to Beijing but it is quite expensive; on the other hand, travelling by trains is an increasingly cost-effective alternative to planes, particularly if I'm going a relatively short distance.
  • Is driving to work popular in your country?
    • Yes, people that I know tend to go to work by car because it's very convenient to drive, but for others without cars, they just take buses or ride an ebike to work because it's cheap and effective
  • Do you think people will drive more in the future?
    • Yes, I believe that as people earn more money and have a better life, they will buy cars for themselves and their families, and most will drive their cars to the office.
